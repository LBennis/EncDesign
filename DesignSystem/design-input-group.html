Details

This demo shows the grouping styles for any/various input types. The goals is to group multiple inputs together, but have them visually present as a unified control.

For example, selecting a date range required 4 inputs for the operator, 2 dates & optional timezone.

Styles

The styles apply primarily to the wrapper and aim to distrupt or overite the input styles minimally. The only adjustment to the inputs is their default resting border colour & their min/max widths. There si then a small divider placed between inputs.
